## Required process

1. Inspect the current behavior and report material conflicts.
2. Propose the file-level change and test approach.
3. Wait for plan approval when risk is High/Critical or the plan changes an approved design.
4. Implement only this task.
5. Run required checks.
6. Perform AI review against the criteria and applicable skills.
7. Report evidence, changed files, limitations, and follow-up items.

## Stop conditions

Stop and ask for a decision when:

- requirements or source documents conflict;
- a public contract, schema, architecture boundary, or production resource must change unexpectedly;
- evidence cannot be produced;
- repeated attempts do not reduce the failure;
- sensitive data or destructive authority is required.
