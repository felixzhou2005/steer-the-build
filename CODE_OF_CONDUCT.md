# Code of Conduct

We want this project to support rigorous disagreement without personal hostility.

Participants are expected to:

- challenge claims with evidence, examples, and clear reasoning;
- distinguish observed results from assumptions and opinions;
- respect confidentiality, privacy, and intellectual property;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and bad-faith repetition;
- accept correction when a source, translation, or technical claim is wrong;
- disclose material affiliations when recommending tools or services.

Maintainers may edit or remove content and restrict participation when behavior undermines safety or constructive collaboration. Serious concerns can be reported privately to the maintainer.
